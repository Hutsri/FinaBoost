Budgeting
Process of itemzing your income and expenses over a period of time.
Budgeting is useful for:
  • Monitoring your income and expenses
  • Planning and preparing for future expenses
  • Responding and adapting to changes in your financial situation.

SMART Goals
Process of setting a financial goal to work upon and making sure they are SMART.

  • S - Specific
  • M - Measurable
  • A - Agreed Upon
  • R - Realistic
  • T - Timed

Investing
Process of allocating money in hope of a bountiful return in the future.
Investing should be done while carefully considering the time frame. They can, thus, be made for:
  • Short-term goals
  • Long-term goals
  • To stay ahead of inflation

Saving
Process of reserving money for manifold requirements.
Contrary to popular notions, saving isn't just meant for handling unprecendented medical bills- it could be fun too!
Why you should save money?
  • For emergency purposes
  • For meeting your long term financial SMART goal
  • For reaping the benefits of compounding interest in the future if you begin early
  • For that week long trip to Europe you've been dreaming of!
